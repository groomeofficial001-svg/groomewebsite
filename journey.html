It began with
observation.

The idea for Groome didn’t begin with technology. It began with noticing something that needed to change.

02

Local salons were
left behind.

While big brands embraced digital transformation, local salons, stylists and grooming businesses still relied on traditional ways. Despite their skill, many struggled with visibility, retention and growth.

03

People were left
guessing.

Choosing a hairstyle came down to guesswork. People weren’t sure what suited them, whom to trust, or where to go. Every visit felt like a gamble.

04

Both sides, same problem.

A lack of clarity and connection. The more we looked, the more we realized both sides were facing the exact same challenge — just from different angles.

05

That’s when
Groome was born.

We set out to bridge that gap — helping people discover styles with confidence while enabling grooming businesses of all sizes to grow and thrive in the digital age.

Because when people know what suits them,

confidence follows.

Our Vision

Confident style for everyone.

We envision a world where anyone can effortlessly discover their best look, express themselves with confidence, and step out feeling like the best version of themselves.

Our Mission

Empowering local businesses to grow.

By bridging the gap between individuals and skilled professionals, we are on a mission to digitize, support, and scale local salons and grooming experts — building a thriving, interconnected ecosystem.
